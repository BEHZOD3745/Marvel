Marvel

A trademark and the main asset of Marvel Worldwide Inc. publishing comics. A division of Marvel Entertainment. Dubbed the "House of Ideas" among comic book fans, Marvel is best known for such comic book series as "Fantastic Four", "X-Men", "The Avengers", "Guardians of the Galaxy", "Spider-Man", "Wolverine", "Iron Man", "Captain America", "Thor", "Hulk", "Deadpool", "Daredevil", "The Punisher", "Doctor Strange", "Black Panther", "Human-Ant and the Wasp", "Captain Marvel", "Venom", "Silver Surfer", "Ghost Rider", "Blade", "Black Widow", "Hawkeye", "Iron Fist" and "Jessica Jones"
